Essay Question

In the past, when students did a university degree they tended to study in their own country. Nowadays, they have more opportunities to study abroad. Do you think the advantages of this development outweigh the disadvantages?

Step 4: Plan Introduction & Conclusion

Draft your introduction by paraphrasing the question and stating your position. Outline your conclusion by summarizing your main points.

Step 5: Plan Body Paragraph 1 (Advantages)

Outline your first main argument. State your point, provide a specific example, and explain how it supports your thesis.

Step 6: Plan Body Paragraph 2 (Disadvantages)

Outline your second main argument (or a counter-argument). Follow the Point, Example, Explanation structure.
