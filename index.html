Essay Question

In the past, when students did a university degree they tended to study in their own country. Nowadays, they have more opportunities to study abroad.

Do you think the advantages of this development outweigh the disadvantages?

Step 4: Plan Introduction & Conclusion

Review these sample introductions and conclusions. Notice how they paraphrase the prompt and state a clear position.

Sample Introduction 1

In an increasingly globalised world, pursuing higher education beyond national borders has become a burgeoning trend. While studying abroad offers students unparalleled exposure and opportunities, it also presents certain far-reaching challenges. In my view, the advantages of international education decidedly outweigh its drawbacks, primarily because it broadens intellectual horizons and enhances global competitiveness.
