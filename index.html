ESSAY QUESTION:-

In the past, when students did a university degree they tended to study in their own country. Nowadays, they have more opportunities to study abroad.

Do you think the advantages of this development outweigh the disadvantages?

Review the key vocabulary for this task. Using a range of words is essential for a high score.

Word/Phrase Meaning Example Sentence
BurgeoningGrowing rapidlyThe burgeoning interest in foreign education reflects global academic integration.
Far-reachingHaving extensive impactThe far-reaching effects of international study influence economies and cultures alike.
AugmentEnhance or increaseStudying abroad can augment one’s academic and interpersonal capabilities.
Cross-cultural competenceAbility to interact effectively with diverse groupsEmployers highly value graduates who demonstrate cross-cultural competence.
